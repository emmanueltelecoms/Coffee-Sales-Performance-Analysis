## Project Overview

## üîë Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The analysis was built around tracking and visualizing the following core metrics:

* **Total Revenue:** Overall financial performance.
* **Count of Transactions:** Total customer activity and volume.
* **Total Sales per Product Category:** Breakdown of core revenue drivers.
* **Transaction per Hour:** Identifying peak demand for staffing optimization.
* **Revenue per Month:** Tracking business growth and seasonal trends.

---

## üìä Key Findings

* **Core Driver:** Coffee is the dominant revenue generator, but low sales in the "Coffee Beans" category indicate a missed opportunity for higher-margin sales.
* **Operational Peak:** The period between **7 AM and 11 AM** is the most crucial for daily operations, demanding optimized staffing and inventory management.
* **Growth Trend:** Revenue shows a positive, upward trend over the analyzed period, confirming market acceptance and business scaling.
